Erika + Joseph November 5, 2016

Burlington Pond, Burlington, ON
Erika and Joseph are loving, genuine, fun, and they simply adore eachother. They first met outside of Chesters in Hamilton for a few drinks. They ended up staying until the bar closed. Erika later said, "If he asks me to marry him tomorrow, I will". Joseph surprised Erika on their first anniversary by flying them out to New York City. After a day of sightseeing, he proposed to her on top of the Empire State Building.

On the morning of their wedding, Erika's friends and family walked with her from the hotel to Chester's. It was only fitting that Erika and Joseph's first look would be outside of the place where they first met.

Erika and Joe planned a truly Canadian wedding. Joseph and his family are Maple Leafs fans, while Erika and her family are Habs fans. At Burlington Pond in front of jersey-clad friends and family, Erika and Joseph tied the knot at centre ice. They walked as husband and wife up the aisle and directly into the dressing room, where they laced up their skates. They skated around the rink to celebrate with family and friends, and enjoyed Tim Hortons hot chocolate and doughnuts.

Their grand entrance at the Waterfront Centre had them bursting through a tunnel of hockey sticks, and into their first dance. Emotional speeches spoke of their love for eachother and how loved they are by their friends and family. As the dance party ensued, Erika, Joseph, and their wedding party snuck out one last time for a sparkler show and night portraits on the docks outside.


Congratulations, Erika and Joseph!
